[] Further information: Tomb of the Kings Entrance to the Tomb of the Kings. The Tomb of the Kings is the royal tomb where the members of the royal family are buried. It is just outside of Alubarna, located underground beneath ordinary tombs, and accessed through a secret staircase. It was destroyed by Nefertari Cobra in an attempt to kill Crocodile, but was rebuilt at a later point.&#91;10&#93;
